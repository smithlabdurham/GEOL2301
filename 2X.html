GEOL2301: Palaeoecosystems

Colonizing the environment: Octave exercise

Creating an assemblage

Stage One: Settlement

Choose whether to stick your hand up or down.

  1. If you match the environment, you colonize:
    • Remain standing
    • your species has one individual in this environment
  2. If you do not match the environment, you die:
    • Sit down
    • You have zero individuals in this environment

Stage Two: Proliferation

Choose whether to stick your hand up or down.

  1. If you match the environment, you reproduce:
    • Remain standing
    • Double your species' individual count in this environment
  2. If you do not match the environment, you die:
    • Sit down
    • Record your final individual count in this environment

Stage Three: Disturbance (×2)

The environment is peturbed!

Everything dies and a new settlement phase begins.

Stage Four: Discovery

The remains of your species are discovered!
